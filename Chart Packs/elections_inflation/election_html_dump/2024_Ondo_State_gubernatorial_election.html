The 2024 Ondo State gubernatorial election will take place on 16 November 2024. Incumbent APC governor Lucky Aiyedatiwa is running for election to a full term. Former deputy governor Agboola Ajayi of the PDP and 15 other party candidates are running against him.[1]

Lucky Aiyedatiwa of the APC was the former deputy governor who became governor after the death of Governor Rotimi Akeredolu in December 2023.[2]

Agboola Ajayi of the PDP was the ZLP gubernatorial candidate in the 2020 gubernatorial election.[3]

Electoral system

[edit]

The Governor of Ondo State is elected using the plurality voting system.
