The 2024 Chilean municipal elections were held on 26 and 27 October 2024. The elections were held on the same day as regional elections.[1] The combined municipal and regional elections had a paricipation rate of 85% which makes it the election with the highest voter turnout in the history of Chile.[2] Among the defeated candidates were a series of high profile politicians like Karla Rubilar, Marcela Cubillos, Iván Poduje and the incumbent mayor of Santiago Irací Hassler who lost to Mario Desbordes.[3]
