UK productivity has stagnated since 2008, but the latest data shows some bright spots: IT, Agriculture and Business Services are all becoming more productive per worker. 
The challenge is that Energy, Health and Finance are becoming less productive per worker, leaving the whole economy productivity growth slow at just 2.8%.

Source: https://www.ons.gov.uk/employmentandlabourmarket/peopleinwork/labourproductivity/articles/ukproductivityintroduction/octobertodecember2025andjulytoseptember2025
